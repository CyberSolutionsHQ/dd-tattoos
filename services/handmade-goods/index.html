Handmade Goods

Forged by local makers - crafted loot for your everyday quest.

Handmade Goods is where we stock small-batch creations from local artists and makers - items that feel personal, practical, and just the right kind of magical. Expect rotating inventory, one-off pieces, and occasional themed drops tied to seasons, events, or collaborations.

Note: Handmade inventory rotates often. If you are after a specific style or maker, message us and we will tell you what is currently in-stock.

Back to Services Contact Us

What you might find (examples)

  • Stickers, pins, patches, and keychains.
  • Prints, mini paintings, and small framed art.
  • Jewelry, accessories, and wearable pieces.
  • Handcrafted decor for desks, shelves, and game tables.
  • Limited-run collab items (when available).

How it works

  • Browse in-studio during business hours.
  • Ask staff about materials, care, and maker details.
  • New drops announced on social media.
  • Interested in something custom? Ask - some makers accept commissions.

Care and quality notes

  • Handmade items may have natural variations - each piece is unique.
  • Keep paper goods dry and away from direct sunlight.
  • For jewelry or accessories, avoid harsh chemicals and heavy moisture.
  • If you are unsure how to care for an item, ask us before you leave.

Pricing and availability

Pricing varies by maker, materials, and complexity. One-of-one items can sell quickly - if you see something you love, it is best to snag it while it is available.

FAQ

Do you accept local makers or vendors?
Yes - when space allows. Send photos, pricing, and a short description of your work.

Do you offer commissions?
Some makers do. Ask in-studio or message us and we will point you to the right person.

Are returns allowed?
Return eligibility depends on the item type and condition. Check the shop's policy on your site for details.
