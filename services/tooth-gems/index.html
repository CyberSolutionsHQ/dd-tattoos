Tooth Gems

A subtle shimmer - clean, stylish, and fantasy-approved.

Tooth gems are small decorative crystals applied to the surface of a tooth for a bright, subtle sparkle. We focus on a clean look, careful placement, and a comfortable experience - perfect for adding a little enchanted loot to your smile.

Note: Tooth gems are cosmetic. If you have dental concerns, sensitivity, or active dental work, consult your dentist first.

Back to Services Book Tooth Gems

What to expect

  • Choose gem size and placement.
  • Tooth surface cleaned and prepared.
  • Gem bonded in place (no drilling).
  • Comfort and alignment check.
  • Aftercare guidance provided.

Aftercare basics

  • Avoid hard or crunchy foods for 24 hours.
  • Avoid whitening strips immediately after application.
  • Brush gently around the gem.
  • Contact the studio if the gem loosens.

Who it's for

Ideal for clients wanting a small statement detail. If you have veneers, crowns, braces, or recent dental work, staff will advise on suitability.

Pricing and booking

Pricing varies by gem style and placement. Contact us for current options or book a session and confirm details during consultation.

FAQ

How long do tooth gems last?
Longevity varies by habits and placement.

Does it hurt?
Application should be comfortable; notify staff if you have sensitivity.

Can it be removed?
Yes - safe removal options will be explained.
