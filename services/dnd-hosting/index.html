D&D Hosting

Gather your party - roll dice, tell stories, and claim glory at our tables.

D&D Hosting is our in-studio tabletop experience for groups who want a dedicated, themed space to play. Whether you are running a weekly campaign or just one epic night, we provide a cozy, atmospheric setting that fits the Dungeon Dweller vibe - perfect for adventurers of all skill levels.

Note: Table availability and event nights vary. Check Events for scheduled games or contact us to reserve a table.

Back to Services View Scheduled Game Nights Reserve a Table

What to expect

  • A reserved play space (when booked).
  • A welcoming, respectful environment for all players.
  • Optional themed nights, one-shots, or community sessions (as available).
  • Staff support for basic questions, schedule, and table rules.

Great for

  • Weekly campaigns.
  • One-shot adventures.
  • New-player intro sessions.
  • Birthday sessions and group celebrations.
  • DM-run games (bring your own DM) or hosted sessions (when offered).

What to bring

  • Your group and your character sheets.
  • Dice, minis, and books (if you use them).
  • Any personal props, maps, or laptops or tablets you prefer.

Table guidelines (high-level)

  • Be respectful and keep the table welcoming.
  • Follow staff instructions and posted rules.
  • Keep drinks and food away from minis, books, and art materials when possible.
  • Clean up at the end of your session.

Pricing and booking

Pricing depends on table size, session length, and whether it is a private reservation or an event night. Book ahead to lock in your time slot.

FAQ

Do you provide a Dungeon Master (DM)?
Sometimes - depending on scheduled events and availability. Most private sessions are bring your own DM.

Can beginners join?
Yes. Community nights are a great place to learn. Check Events for beginner-friendly sessions.

Can we host other tabletop games?
Absolutely - ask us about availability and any table rules for your game type.
