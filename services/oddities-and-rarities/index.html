Oddities & Rarities

Curated curios - bones, bottles, bugs, and beautiful weird.

Oddities & Rarities is our little in-house curiosity cabinet: a rotating collection of strange, spooky, and fantasy-friendly finds. Whether you are hunting a shelf piece for your lair or a gift for a fellow adventurer, you will find treasures that feel pulled straight from a dungeon chest.

Note: Inventory changes often. If you are looking for something specific, message us before you travel.

Back to Services Contact Us

What you'll find (examples)

  • Curios and collectibles (display pieces, trinkets, loot items).
  • Art prints, pins, patches, and small decor.
  • Limited drops and guest or vendor items (when available).
  • Seasonal themed finds and shop exclusives.

How it works

  • Browse in-studio during open hours.
  • Ask staff about materials, origin, and care.
  • New items rotate in regularly - check socials for drops.
  • If you see it and love it, snag it (one-of-one items go fast).

Care and handling basics

  • Keep display pieces away from moisture and direct sunlight.
  • Handle fragile items gently (glass, resin, delicate parts).
  • For natural or organic materials, keep in a cool, dry place.
  • If you are unsure, ask us - care varies by item.

Pricing and availability

Prices vary by item rarity and artist or vendor. Limited drops may sell out quickly. Contact us for current availability or to ask about upcoming releases.

FAQ

Do you accept vendor items or local makers?
Yes - when space allows. Reach out with photos, pricing, and quantities.

Can you hold an item for me?
Holds may be possible for a short time depending on demand - message us ASAP.

Are items returnable?
For hygiene and fragile or unique inventory reasons, returns may be limited. Check the shop policy on your site.
