Henna

Temporary artistry - ornate lines, bold symbols, and enchanted patterns.

Henna is a natural dye used to create temporary skin art with rich, warm tones. It is perfect for special events, trying a design before committing to a tattoo, or just adding a little magical flair for the weekend. Designs can range from delicate and minimal to full, intricate fantasy patterns.

Note: Results vary by skin type, placement, and aftercare. If you have sensitive skin or allergies, tell us before we begin.

What to expect

  • Quick chat about design ideas, size, and placement.
  • Design is applied carefully by hand (freehand or stencil, depending on style).
  • Dry time guidance so the design sets properly.
  • Aftercare basics to help the stain develop and last longer.

Design options (examples)

  • Minimal symbols, runes, and sigils.
  • Floral, ornamental, and geometric patterns.
  • Fantasy motifs (dragons, moons, daggers, crests).
  • Hand or arm accents or larger statement pieces.

Aftercare basics

  • Keep the paste on as long as recommended (longer can deepen the stain).
  • Avoid water while the paste is setting or drying.
  • Once paste is removed, avoid heavy scrubbing for 24 hours.
  • Moisturize lightly and avoid chlorine if possible.
  • Natural fading happens over time - this is normal.

How long does it last?

Most henna designs fade gradually over 1-2 weeks, depending on placement, skin type, and daily routine. Hands and areas washed often may fade faster.

Pricing and booking

Pricing depends on size, detail level, and placement. Contact us for current options, or book a session and we will confirm the design scope with you.

FAQ

Is henna safe?
We use skin-safe henna products. If you have allergies or sensitive skin, let us know beforehand.

Will it look dark immediately?
Henna typically deepens over the first 24-48 hours as the stain develops.

Can I bring a reference design?
Absolutely - bring inspiration and we will adapt it to fit your placement and style.
