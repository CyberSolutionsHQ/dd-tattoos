Art Classes

Level up your craft - learn, practice, and create with the guild.

Our Art Classes are built for beginners and creatives who want structure, guidance, and a fun studio atmosphere. We run themed sessions that fit the Dungeon Dweller vibe - fantasy sketches, character design, ink-style illustration, and more - while keeping the fundamentals easy to follow.

Note: Class topics and schedules rotate. Check Events for upcoming sessions or message us to ask what is next.

Back to Services View Upcoming Classes Book / Reserve

What to expect

  • A guided, step-by-step session led by an instructor.
  • Supplies info provided ahead of time (some classes include materials).
  • A relaxed environment where questions are welcome.
  • You leave with a finished piece (or a strong start) and practice tips.

Class types (examples)

  • Beginner drawing fundamentals (shape, line, shading).
  • Fantasy character or creature sketch nights.
  • Ink and illustration sessions (tattoo-inspired techniques).
  • Color basics (markers, watercolor, or digital - depending on the class).
  • Mini-workshops (hands, faces, daggers, dragons, runes, and more).

What to bring

  • Bring a sketchbook and pencil set (if listed for that class).
  • Optional: your favorite pens or markers.
  • If a class includes supplies, we will say so in the event listing.

Skill levels

We welcome all levels. If you are brand new, we will keep it friendly. If you are experienced, you will still get useful technique focus and feedback.

Pricing and booking

Pricing varies by class length and whether supplies are included. Reserve a spot through our Events page or contact us to confirm the next available session.

FAQ

Do I need experience to attend?
Nope - beginner-friendly options are common.

Are supplies included?
Sometimes. Each class listing will specify what is included and what to bring.

Can kids attend?
Depends on the class and event rules. Check the listing or message us first.
