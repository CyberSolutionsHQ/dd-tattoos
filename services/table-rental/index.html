Table Rental

Reserve a corner of the tavern - your campaign, your crew, your time.

Table Rental is for groups who want a dedicated space to play tabletop games, run a campaign, host a casual meetup, or spend a few focused hours crafting, painting minis, or planning their next quest. It is a simple way to guarantee your party has a spot - especially on busy nights.

Note: Availability depends on the calendar and event schedule. Booking ahead is strongly recommended.

FAQ

Can we walk in without a reservation?
Sometimes - if there is an open table. Reservations guarantee a spot.

Can we bring our own food or drinks?
Rules vary by night and local requirements. Ask ahead and check the event listing if applicable.

Can we decorate the table or bring props?
Yes - within reason. No mess that damages surfaces. Ask staff if you have something special planned.
