Paint & Sip

A cozy tavern night - paint, laugh, and leave with a masterpiece.

Paint & Sip is a guided painting session with relaxed energy and Dungeon Dweller flavor. Whether you are a total beginner or you have painted before, you will get step-by-step direction, a fun theme, and plenty of room to make it your own. Come solo, bring friends, or roll in with your whole party.

Note: This is an event-style experience. Themes, dates, and age rules depend on the specific listing.

What to expect

  • A guided painting session (beginner-friendly).
  • Theme-based artwork (fantasy, spooky, seasonal, or custom concepts).
  • A supportive, low-pressure environment.
  • You take home your finished piece.

What's included (typical)

  • Canvas or paper and basic supplies (varies by event).
  • Instructor guidance and reference materials.
  • Setup and cleanup handled by staff.

What to bring

  • Yourself and your party (optional).
  • Any personal brushes or paint you prefer (optional).
  • If refreshments are offered, the event listing will explain rules and requirements.

Who it's for

Perfect for date nights, friend groups, birthdays, and anyone who wants a creative night out - no art experience required.

Pricing and booking

Pricing depends on the event theme and what is included. Reserve a seat through our Events page or contact us to confirm the next Paint & Sip date.

FAQ

Do I need painting experience?
Not at all - these sessions are designed for beginners.

Can we paint something different?
Usually the class follows a theme, but you can add personal flair. Ask at the start.

Is this BYOB?
Rules vary by event and local requirements. Check the specific event listing for details.
