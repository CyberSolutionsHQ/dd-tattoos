Game Tournaments

Prove your skill - brackets, battles, and bragging rights.

Game Tournaments are our organized competition nights for tabletop and card games - sometimes serious, sometimes chaotic, always fun. Whether you are chasing a win streak or just here for the vibes, tournaments are a great way to meet new players and build community in the Dungeon Dweller guildhall.

Note: Tournament games and schedules rotate. Check Events for current brackets, formats, and rules.

Back to Services View Upcoming Tournaments Register / Reserve

What to expect

  • A structured event with start time, rounds, and basic rules.
  • Staff-organized brackets or pairings.
  • A friendly, respectful competitive atmosphere.
  • Optional prizes, store credit, or themed rewards (when offered).

Games we may run (examples)

  • Trading card games (TCG nights).
  • Board game brackets.
  • Mini skirmish formats (as announced).
  • One-off specialty events and seasonal tournaments.

How to join

  • Check the Events page for upcoming tournaments.
  • Reserve a spot if registration is required.
  • Show up early for check-in (recommended).
  • Follow posted rules and staff instructions.

House rules and conduct

  • Respect players, staff, and the space.
  • No cheating, harassment, or disruptive behavior.
  • Keep the play area clean and clear between rounds.
  • Rules disputes follow the event organizer's final call.

Pricing and registration

Entry fees (if any) and prize details vary by event. Some tournaments are free, others require registration or a small entry fee depending on prizes and setup.

FAQ

Do I need to bring my own game or deck?
Usually yes, unless the event listing says supplies are provided.

Are beginners welcome?
Yes - some events are beginner-friendly. Check the event description for skill level.

What if I am late?
Late arrivals may forfeit a round depending on the bracket format. Arrive early if possible.
